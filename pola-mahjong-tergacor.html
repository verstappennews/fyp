About Us:

Who We Are:

Myers Electric & Automation Inc. has been in business since 1983, serving individuals and companies in Oregon and Southwest Washington. Providing excellent service to our customers is at the core of all that we do. We believe that listening carefully to what is important to our customers is part of what builds the strong relationships that we value greatly as a company. We pride ourselves for being a company built upon strong character, integrity and ingenuity; making it the driving force behind every decision we make, from concept to completion. From the beginning, our company has always been dedicated to being leaders in the realm of creative thinking and problem solving. No job is too difficult. We will find a way to overcome any challenge to accomplish the given goal; with the highest quality workmanship, top quality material, on time and on budget. With experience in all areas of our industry for over 30 years, we have a wealth of expertise to assist companies expand, improve profitability, increase production, maintain solid functional equipment, and minimize downtime.  Whether working with a company, or a homeowner, our focus is always on providing peace of mind and satisfaction through our outstanding workmanship. It is a priority to us, and we work hard to make sure all work is done right and done safely.  Our goal, in every project, is to set the standard for the quality of work within the electrical industry, striving for excellence in all that we do and exceeding all expectations.

Goals:

We really care about the people and companies we serve. It is because of this deep commitment that we strive to provide the highest quality workmanship. 


Our Goals:

  • To develop and maintain long term relationships with our customers and surpass their expectations.
  • To set the standard for the quality of work within the electrical industry and be excellent in all that we do.
  • To be leaders and set the standard for the quality of work within the   electrical industry.
  • To solve problems and overcome challenges while staying on time and on budget. 
  • To maintain a unique culture built on integrity that is enjoyable and rewarding to both our team members and customers.
  • To maintain a high standard of safety, quality and efficiency in each job to achieve the greatest customer satisfaction.
  • To maintain a highly trained team; always continuing to learn and prefect our skills.   

Services:

We offer a full range of services to meet all your electrical needs. Whether industrial, commercial or residential; we have a highly trained team ready to accomplish your goals.  From a single outlet in your home to a large industrial service, and everything in between; we can help. No matter the difficulty or complexity of your project; we excel at solving problems for our customers.

Our Team:

Our journeymen electricians are highly trained in the most current NEC (National Electric Code), NFPA 70E; Standard for Electrical Safety in the Workplace ® and are up to speed with today’s ever-changing technological trends. Each one has been hand-picked and brings with them an abundance of knowledge and experience into each project.  They are required to regularly attend continuing education classes to ensure that they are always operating within the most current standards, and some have also completed additional specialized training within our industry. Our apprentices receive the highest quality training and are monitored closely by our experienced journeyman to learn firsthand the skills and knowledge necessary to becoming a successful electrician.
